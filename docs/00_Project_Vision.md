# HeartSync Project Vision

## Problem Statement

Many clinics and hospitals manage patient records, appointments, reports, and prescriptions using disconnected systems.

This creates delays, inefficiencies, and poor patient experience.

---

## Solution

HeartSync is an AI-Powered Cardiology CRM and Patient Management Platform that unifies the complete patient journey in a single system.

---

## Users

- Patients
- Doctors
- Nurses
- Lab Technicians
- Pharmacists
- Administrators

---

## Core Modules

1. Authentication
2. Patient Management
3. Doctor Management
4. Appointment System
5. Laboratory Management
6. Pharmacy Management
7. Dashboard & Analytics
8. AI Risk Prediction
9. Symptom Checker
10. Azure Cloud Deployment

---

## Goal

Build a production-style healthcare platform while learning:
