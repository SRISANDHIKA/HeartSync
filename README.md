# HeartSync 💙

## AI-Powered Cardiology CRM & Patient Management Platform

HeartSync is a comprehensive healthcare management platform designed to streamline the complete cardiology patient journey.

---

## 🎯 Vision

To connect patients, doctors, nurses, laboratory technicians, pharmacy staff, and administrators through a single intelligent healthcare ecosystem.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### Authentication & Authorization
- User Registration
- Login & Logout
- Role-Based Access Control

### Patient Management
- Patient Profiles
- Medical History
- Vitals Tracking

### Doctor Management
- Doctor Profiles
- Availability Scheduling

### Appointment System
- Appointment Booking
- Smart Slot Engine
- Follow-Up Scheduling

### Laboratory Management
- Test Requests
- Report Uploads
- Diagnostic Tracking

### Pharmacy Management
- Prescription Management
- Medicine Requests

### Dashboard & Analytics
- Patient Statistics
- Appointment Analytics
- Operational Insights

### AI Features
- Cardiac Risk Prediction
- Symptom Checker
- Health Assistant
